(se)QuenceML

Stretchable transposable sequenceable musical notation

Allow notes to be in a key as numbers (plus octaves) or specified
Sequences and random possibilities
Adjustable timings and stretchings
Base note lengths on 12
Optional notes can disappear or rearrange in processing, with hints for how to handle
Multiple voices, able to shift together
Option to specify basso continuo equivalent, have computer calculate path (actually use BC? Or figure out something clearer?)
Randomize which things?  Timing?  Appearance?  Voice?


Initial output as MIDI, eventual output as MusicXML with measures calculated

WEBMIDI.js as target for initial prototyping
